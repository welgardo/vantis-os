## 🌑 What is Vantis?

Vantis is not a fork with a new wallpaper slapped on top.

It's a security-first Linux distribution, built on Arch, designed from the very first line to be the daily driver of someone who thinks about systems the way an attacker — and a defender — does. Fast. Minimal. Deliberate. Nothing running that you didn't choose to run.

Most distributions in this space repackage the same public toolset everyone already has. Vantis does that too — but that's just the floor, not the ceiling. Underneath the familiar surface is a growing collection of **original tooling**, built specifically for this system, engineered in-house, and shaped by real workflows rather than a checklist of "tools every distro should have."

This isn't a weekend build. Vantis is a long-term commitment — a system meant to be developed, broken, rebuilt, and shipped, year after year.

## ⚡ Why Arch?

Because control shouldn't be optional.

- **Rolling release** — the newest kernel, the newest tools, the newest CVE data, always. No waiting six months for a point release.
- **Minimal by default** — nothing pre-installed that you didn't ask for. No bloat pretending to be a feature.
- **AUR from day one** — the largest community package ecosystem in Linux, available immediately.
- **Full transparency** — every part of the system is understood, configured, and owned on purpose. Nothing is a black box.

Arch isn't chosen because it's trendy. It's chosen because it gets out of the way.

## 🛠️ Original tooling

This is where Vantis stops being "just another Arch spin."

Alongside the standard, battle-tested toolkit you'd expect on any serious security distribution, Vantis ships — and will continue to ship — a set of **tools built entirely in-house**. Not forks. Not wrappers around someone else's project with a new coat of paint. Original software, written for this system, solving problems the way their creator actually encounters them.

These tools aren't announced in advance. They arrive when they're ready, and they stay exclusive to Vantis. Some are small utilities that quietly make a workflow faster. Others are full systems built from scratch, addressing gaps that nothing else on the market fills.

The exact list grows over time — and that's the point. Vantis is not a finished product being maintained. It's a living toolkit, actively expanding.

## 🧭 Philosophy

A distribution is a statement about how you believe a system should behave. Vantis is built on a small number of non-negotiable beliefs:

**Do nothing you weren't asked to do.**
No telemetry. No bloat. No guessing what the user "probably wants."

**Stay current, always.**
A rolling release model means the tools you rely on are never stale by the time you need them.

**Build what doesn't exist yet.**
If a tool already does the job well, Vantis won't reinvent it. If it doesn't exist, Vantis will build it.

**Be understood, not just used.**
Every component should be something its user could explain, not just click.

## 👤 Who this is for

Vantis is being built for people who don't want their operating system to be a mystery box — security researchers, students who actually want to understand what's running under the hood, and anyone who'd rather configure a system on purpose than inherit someone else's defaults.

If you want something that "just works" without asking questions, there are gentler distributions than this one. If you want a system that respects your intelligence and gives you the tools to match it, you're in the right place.
